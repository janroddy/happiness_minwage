Notes on the data:
Happiness Score
31 different metrics were studied when determining which states were the happiest. These metrics included the number of work hours, job security, adult depression, income growth, and even the weather.
Income Inequality/ Gini Coefficient
A score of "0" on the Gini coefficient represents complete equality, i.e., every person has the same income. A score of 1 would represent complete inequality.
